# Workplace Safety Policy

## Purpose
To foster a safe, hazard‑free environment across all work sites.

## Safety Principles
- Everyone is responsible for maintaining safety.
- Hazards must be reported immediately.
- Safety equipment must be used as required.

## Key Areas
- Fire safety and evacuation plans.
- Ergonomic workstation setup.
- Incident and near‑miss reporting.

## Emergency Procedures
- Know exit routes.
- Follow instructions from emergency coordinators.
- Participate in drills.

## Documentation
Supervisors maintain incident logs for compliance tracking.

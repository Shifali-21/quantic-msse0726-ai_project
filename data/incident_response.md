# Incident Response Policy

## Purpose
To guide employees through identification, reporting, and mitigation of security incidents.

## Incident Types
- Malware or ransomware infection
- Unauthorized data access
- Lost or stolen device
- Phishing attacks

## Response Process
1. Identify and isolate affected systems.
2. Report immediately to IT Security.
3. Preserve evidence.
4. Follow recovery procedures.

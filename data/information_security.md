# Information Security Policy

## Purpose
To safeguard all organizational data and digital infrastructure from misuse, breach, or unauthorized access.

## Responsibilities
- Employees must protect their credentials.
- IT ensures secure system configuration.
- Managers enforce security compliance.

## Requirements
- Multi-factor authentication for system access.
- Encryption of sensitive data at rest and in transit.
- Strong password creation and secure storage.
- Prohibition of unapproved thirdâ€‘party apps.

## Operational Controls
- Network monitoring and threat detection.
- Regular patching of servers and endpoints.
- Backup and disaster recovery procedures.
- Logging of access and security events.

## Incident Reporting
All incidents must be reported immediately to the Security Response Team.

## Enforcement
Non-compliance may result in disciplinary action.

# Remote & Hybrid Work Policy

## Purpose
To ensure employees working remotely do so securely, productively, and professionally.

## Workspace Standards
- Maintain a quiet, private workstation.
- Protect visible documents and screens.
- Use a company‑issued or approved device.

## Security Requirements
- Use company VPN when accessing internal systems.
- Do not allow family members to use corporate devices.
- Secure home Wi‑Fi with strong encryption.

## Communication Expectations
- Attend virtual meetings on time.
- Use video conferencing appropriately.
- Set working hours that mirror on‑site requirements.

## Support
IT provides remote troubleshooting and device management.

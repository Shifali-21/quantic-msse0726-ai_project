# Anti‑Harassment & Anti‑Discrimination Policy

## Purpose
To maintain a safe, inclusive workplace free from harassment, discrimination, and retaliation.

## Definitions
- **Harassment:** Any unwelcome conduct creating an intimidating environment.
- **Discrimination:** Unfair treatment based on protected characteristics.

## Examples of Harassment
- Offensive jokes or comments.
- Unwanted physical contact.
- Repeated unwelcome invitations.
- Posting derogatory material in shared spaces.

## Reporting
Employees may report concerns:
- Directly to HR
- Through an anonymous reporting line
- To any supervisor

## Investigation
1. HR reviews all complaints.
2. Interviews and documentation collected.
3. Determination letter issued with next steps.

## Support
Employees may request accommodations, schedule adjustments, or wellness referrals.

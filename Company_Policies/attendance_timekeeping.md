# Attendance & Timekeeping Policy

## Purpose
To ensure accurate reporting of work hours and consistent employee availability.

## Standards
- Standard working hours: Monday–Friday, 9 AM–5 PM.
- All employees must log hours via the timekeeping system.
- Overtime requires pre‑approval from supervisors.

## Absence Reporting
- Notify supervisor at least one hour prior to shift.
- Submit documentation for medical or emergency leave.
- Repeated tardiness may lead to corrective actions.

## Records Management
Timekeeping data is stored securely by HR for compliance audits.

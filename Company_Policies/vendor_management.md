# Vendor Management & Third‑Party Risk Policy

## Purpose
To ensure third‑party partners meet security, compliance, and operational standards.

## Vendor Evaluation
- Assess service quality and risk level.
- Review data privacy practices.
- Confirm contract terms (confidentiality, security, breach notifications).

## Monitoring
- Annual vendor reviews.
- Compliance attestation where required.

## Termination
Ensure secure return or destruction of organizational data when ending a vendor relationship.

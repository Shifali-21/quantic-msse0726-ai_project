# Acceptable Use of Technology Policy

## Purpose
To define proper and safe use of organizational technology resources.

## Authorized Use
- Accessing systems necessary for job duties.
- Using corporate email for business communication.
- Engaging in professional collaboration using approved tools.

## Prohibited Use
- Installing unauthorized applications.
- Accessing inappropriate, unlawful, or harmful websites.
- Engaging in personal commercial activities.
- Attempting to bypass network restrictions.

## Data Handling
- Store files only on approved drives.
- Do not upload sensitive data to personal cloud storage.
- Follow retention and archival guidelines.

## Monitoring
The organization may log and monitor network usage to ensure compliance.

# Performance Review & Development Policy

## Purpose
To support continuous improvement and professional growth.

## Components
- Annual evaluation
- Mid‑year progress review
- Goal setting and career development planning

## Evaluation Areas
- Job knowledge
- Quality of work
- Collaboration and communication
- Initiative and problem‑solving

## Process
1. Employee self‑assessment.
2. Manager evaluation.
3. Performance meeting.
4. Documentation stored in HR systems.

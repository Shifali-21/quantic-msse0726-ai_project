# Code of Conduct Policy

## Purpose
This policy outlines the ethical, professional, and interpersonal standards expected from all members of the organization.

## Scope
All employees, contractors, consultants, interns, and temporary workers.

## Guiding Principles
- Integrity in all business interactions.
- Respectful and inclusive communication.
- Zero tolerance for harassment or retaliation.
- Commitment to confidentiality and responsible information handling.

## Expected Behaviors
- Use professional, respectful language in all settings.
- Act with honesty when representing the organization.
- Address disagreements constructively.
- Demonstrate cultural awareness and sensitivity.

## Prohibited Behaviors
- Harassment (verbal, visual, physical, digital).
- Discriminatory remarks or favoritism.
- Sharing confidential information without authorization.
- Fraud, misrepresentation, or unethical conduct.

## Reporting & Resolution
1. Report issues to HR or the Ethics Office.
2. Investigations begin promptly with confidentiality maintained.
3. Corrective and preventive actions follow investigative outcomes.

## Review Cycle
Reviewed annually by HR and Compliance.

# Social Media & Public Communication Policy

## Purpose
To protect organizational reputation and ensure safe communication practices.

## Personal Use
- Employees may use personal accounts but must not imply official representation.
- Confidential information must never be shared.

## Professional Use
- Only designated staff may post on official channels.
- All messaging must align with brand and communication guidelines.

## Misconduct
Posting harmful, discriminatory, or inappropriate content may lead to disciplinary action.
